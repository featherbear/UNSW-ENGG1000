Rough Guide to Communicating in Engineering

 

Below are some on-line tutorials on engineering report writing.
HIGHLY  recommended for all writers! 

Design Reports - Online Tutorials

This IWRITE tutorial can help you  prepare your engineering design reports, and contains descriptions and examples of report sections and a few short exercises.
The IWRITE tutorials were developed in collaboration with engineering academics and learning advisors from UNSW & USYD and funded by The Office of Learning and Teaching (OLT).

 

Writing Clearly Tutorial

This IWRITE  tutorial will show you how to express your ideas clearly . Completing the exercises would be great practice for ANY writer.
 

Using visuals in reports

This WRiSE tutorial will show you how to use visuals in your technical reports . 
WRiSE was developed by UNSW & USYD engineering academics and learning advisors and was funded by ALTC.
