What goes in an EIS?

Friday, 13 April 2018

12:50 PM

 

 

 

The EP&A Regulation 2000 outlines the contents of an EIS

  • Summary of the EIS
  • Objectives of the development
  • Analysis of alternatives
  • Analysis of the development
    • Especially environmental impact
