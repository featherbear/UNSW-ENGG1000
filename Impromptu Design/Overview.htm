What is impromptu design?

 

Impromptu - meaning "without preparation", and
Design - meaning the "development of a project"

 

Objective - To work as a team and develop an object to meet certain specifications with only given items.

 

Why - This activity helps to build teamwork and communication skills, crucial for a successfully engineered product
